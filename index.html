About Me

I am a UI/UX designer and developer skilled in HTML, CSS/Sass, JavaScript, Adobe Creative Suite and Sketch as well as template platforms including Wordpress. My extensive experience and background in graphic design allow me to bridge the gap between design and development to create the most beautiful, and highly functional, websites and mobile apps.

I am currently the lead creative director (REMOTE) for a tech company for pets and pet-lovers based in Japan as well as a web designer for a production company based in Vancouver, Canada.
